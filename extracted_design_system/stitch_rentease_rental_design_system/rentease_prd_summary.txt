# Product Requirements Document (PRD)
## RentEase – Furniture & Appliance Rental Platform
**Version:** 1.0 | **Status:** Draft

### 1. Executive Summary
RentEase is a full-stack web-based rental platform designed to provide affordable and flexible furniture and appliance rental services for students, working professionals, and families.

### 2. Problem Statement
Purchasing furniture and appliances for temporary stays results in significant financial investment, transportation difficulties, and maintenance responsibilities. Current services often have high deposits and poor inventory visibility.

### 3. Target Audience
- College Students
- Working Professionals
- Temporary Residents
- Corporate Employees
- Furniture/Appliance Vendors

### 4. Key Features (In Scope)
- **Customer:** Product catalog, rental plans, cart, checkout, delivery/pickup scheduling, maintenance requests, rental extension.
- **Vendor:** Inventory management, pricing updates, maintenance handling, delivery tracking.
- **Admin:** User/vendor management, order monitoring, reports & analytics.

### 5. Technical Stack
- **Frontend:** React.js, TypeScript, Tailwind CSS.
- **Backend:** Node.js, Express.js, MongoDB.

### 6. Core Modules
1. **Authentication:** Role-based access (Customer, Vendor, Admin).
2. **Product Management:** Search, filter, and detailed view for Furniture & Appliances.
3. **Shopping Cart & Checkout:** Calculate rental cost and security deposits.
4. **Maintenance Module:** Request tracking and resolution workflow.
5. **Dashboards:** Dedicated interfaces for all three user roles.
